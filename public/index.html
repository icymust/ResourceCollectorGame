Collect resources on the field to earn points. The player with the most points wins!

🎵 Audio Features

  • Sound Effects: Coin pickup sounds when collecting resources

  • Toggle Option: Enable/disable sound effects completely
  • Test Sound: Preview audio before playing

📋 Game Rules

  • Move around the 20x20 grid to collect resources
  • Resources respawn randomly across the field every 2 seconds
  • Special Effects: Some resources grant temporary powers
  • Danger Zones: Time Bombs explode after 1-6 seconds (risk vs reward)
  • Hidden Traps: Freeze Traps are invisible until triggered
  • Strategy: Use Teleport to escape danger, Magnet to collect safely
  • Resources disappear after 7 seconds if not collected
  • Game ends when time runs out
  • Player with highest score wins!

⚠️ Traps & Danger Zones

💣
Time Bomb

Visible: Yes, with pulsing red animation

Timer: Explodes in 1-6 seconds (random)

Explosion Radius: 3 tiles in all directions (Manhattan distance)

Effects:

  • ✅ Deactivate: Step on it before explosion → +4 points
  • 💥 Explode: -3 points to all players in radius
  • 🔥 Chain Reaction: Destroys all resources in blast radius
🧊
Freeze Trap

Visible: No, completely invisible stealth trap

Duration: Freezes player for 4 seconds

Effect Radius: Single tile (only affects the player who steps on it)

Effects:

  • ❄️ Immobilization: Cannot move for 4 seconds
  • 🧊 Visual Effect: Ice crystals and blue border around player
  • ⚡ No Damage: Only blocks movement, no point loss
📏 Radius Explanation:

Manhattan Distance: Calculated as |x1-x2| + |y1-y2|
